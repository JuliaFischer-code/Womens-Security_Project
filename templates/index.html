Safety Protocol

Do you feel followed, harassed, or threatened?

  • Share your location with a trusted contact.
  • Move to a safe place (busy area, shop, public building).
  • Activate SOS: Request help by a trusted contact or emergency services.
  • Send a code word, if you can't speak.
  • After the situation: Document the incident (e.g., photos) and report to authorities.
